About Through The Kitchen Hole

Through The Kitchen Hole is a rock band based out of Eau Claire, WI. Starting out in high school as a casual garage cover band, TTKH has since grown more eager as they continue to play and expand on their shared love of music. TTKH plays at many venues across the Chippewa Valley such as The Plus, Jake's Supper Club, and the Relay For Life. They also competed in the Rockonsin garage band competition at Summerfest in 2018, and the Bitter Jester Music Festival in 2019.


TTKH has an album titled Through The Kitchen Hole out on all major streaming platforms. The album features four of their favorite cover songs as well as one original song titled "Transitions", which was written in 2018. They are currently working on a new album of all original tracks.

Abby Berthiaume

Abby Berthiaume is studying Early Childhood Special Education at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. She started singing in choirs in middle school and continued throughout high school. She took voice lessons for 5 years under different teachers with her main vocal coach being Luke Otto. She has been in Through The Kitchen Hole since the formation of the band in 2018.

Braedon Laundrie

Braedon began playing the drums at nine years old in Eau Claire, WI, where he took lessons under Ron Guard, and later under Jim Gallagher at age 17. He has played in jazz bands, jazz combos, rock bands, and orchestra pits throughout Wisconsin. Braedon is currently studying at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire. Braedon officially joined Through The Kitchen Hole in March of 2019.

Katherine Seeger

Katherine Seeger is a classical and jazz violinist and bluegrass fiddler from Eau Claire, Wisconsin. She began studying violin with her father at the age of 4. She was Concert Master and Principal Second of the Chippewa Valley Youth Symphony and Wisconsin State Honors Orchestra. She was also a featured soloist with the Moscow Ballet. She is completing her Bachelor's in Music Violin Performance at the University of Minnesota as a student of Kyu-Young Kim. She has previously studied with Young-Nam Kim.

Toby Moszer

Toby Moszer started playing French horn when he was 11, and dropped that in favor of guitar when he was 14. He was originally self taught, and then took lessons mostly under Paul Brandt. Toby joined Jazz I at Memorial High School his junior and senior year, and joined Through The Kitchen Hole when it was created in 2018. Toby is currently studying computer science at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities.

Zach Menter

Zachary Menter started playing guitar when he was 8 years old. Originally teaching himself and learning from his dad, he eventually took lessons for a few years under Lucas Fischer. Zach was in choir all through middle school and high school and is one of the founding members of TTKH. He is currently studying computer science at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire.
